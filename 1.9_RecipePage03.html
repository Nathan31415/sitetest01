Bald Eagle Bratwurst

Ingredients

  • 1 Bald eagle
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup AMERICAN beer
  • 1 ½ teaspoons black pepper, ground
  • 1 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon sage, ground
  • 5 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder

Directions

Use a quarter inch grinding plate to grind your Bald Eagle

In a small mixing bowl beat eggs and beer together until well blended, making sure that the mixture stays american throughout the whole process. Add dry ingredients slowly.

In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground eagle and wet mixture until well incorporated. Cover in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, up to 24 hours; the longer the better. Once refrigerated, stuff your meat into casings, grill, and enjoy! best with a side of Patriotic Potatoes.
